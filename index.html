About us

Started by two people who wanted to own the whole stack.

AURUX started as a simple frustration: every AI project we touched depended on infrastructure we didn't control. So we built our own — a compute farm we maintain ourselves, paired with an AI studio that builds directly on top of it.

The name comes from "aurum," gold — the idea that intelligence, like gold, has to be grown, refined, and tended, not mined and dumped on someone's desk.
